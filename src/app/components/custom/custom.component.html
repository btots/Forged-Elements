Custom Order Information

Because of the nature of modern blacksmithing, it’s hard to point to any one type of job that I specialize in, but here are some of the services I offer regularly:

  • Railings
  • Handrails
  • Fire pits
  • Fire screens
  • Fireplace tools
  • Gates
  • Door/cabinet/household hardware
  • Garden ornamentation
  • Tools
  • Furniture
  • Art
  • Kitchen/cookout tools

But if you don’t see what you’re after in that list, fear not! That’s by no means a comprehensive list. Frankly, I’m willing to tackle just about any job I think I can fit in the forge. If you’ve got a weird idea you’ve been kicking around, reach out and I’ll see what I can do for you! I’ve made thuribles and I’ve made theft-resistant drain covers, so chances are you won’t surprise me (but I welcome a good challenge).
